App Blocking

Easily take control by blocking distracting apps. FlowBuddy allows you to set up custom blocklists for apps that tend to derail your productivity. With just a few taps, you can create a distraction-free environment tailored to your needs.

Unblock for 5 or 10 min

When you try to access a blocked app, you're offered a 5 or 10-minute unblock option. This brief window promotes mindful usage, helping you stay focused and achieve specific tasks without slipping into prolonged browsing. It balances productivity and flexibility, letting you use apps when needed while maintaining focus and digital well-being.

Learn on every Open

Create Flashcards with AI and review them on every App open. Transform idle moments into learning opportunities. FlowBuddy's AI-powered flashcard system helps you retain information and make the most of your screen time, turning app opens into micro-learning sessions.
