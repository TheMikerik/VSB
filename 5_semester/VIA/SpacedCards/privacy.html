Spaced Cards Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 1, 2025

Welcome to Spaced Cards! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your personal information when you use our flashcard app ("App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled in accordance with applicable data protection laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

1. Information Collected

We collect various types of information from you when you use our App:

  • Personal Information: This includes your name and email address, which you provide when you create an account.
  • Usage Information: We collect data about how you use the App, such as which flashcards you study, your performance metrics, and app usage patterns. This helps us improve the App and provide a better user experience.
  • Device Information: We collect information about your device, including the type of device, operating system, and app version. This information is used to ensure compatibility and to provide technical support.
  • Payment Information: If you make in-app purchases or subscribe to premium features, your payment information is collected and processed by Superwall, our third-party payment processor. Superwall has its own privacy policy and is compliant with relevant data protection regulations (Superwall Privacy). We do not store your payment information.
  • User-Generated Content: If you create your own flashcards, the content you provide may be stored in our systems. We do not share this content with third parties unless required by law or with your consent.
  • IP Address: We may collect your IP address for security purposes and to analyze traffic and usage patterns.
  • AI Feature Data: If you use AI features in the App, your input and the generated content are collected for processing by Google's Gemini AI.

2. Use of Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the App's functionality.
  • To analyze and understand how you use the App, including through analytics provided by Posthog (Posthog Privacy), which helps us improve its features and user experience.
  • To process payments and manage subscriptions for premium features via Superwall (Superwall Privacy).
  • To communicate with you about updates, new features, and support.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements.
  • To provide AI-powered features, where AI feature data is shared with Google's Gemini AI.

3. Data Sharing

We may share your personal information with the following third parties:

  • Service Providers (Data Processors): We use several service providers to help us operate the App and provide our services. These service providers act as data processors on our behalf and are bound by contractual obligations to handle your data in accordance with our instructions and applicable data protection laws. They include:
    • Posthog: For user interaction tracking and analytics, EU-hosted (Posthog Privacy).
    • Supabase: For database services, EU-hosted (Supabase Privacy).
    • Sentry: For error tracking and debugging, EU-hosted (Sentry Privacy).
    • PowerSync: For syncing data across devices, assumed EU-hosted (PowerSync Privacy).
    • Superwall: For handling in-app purchases and subscriptions, with GDPR compliance (Superwall Privacy).
    • UpStash: For rate limiting and data storage, with their own privacy policy (UpStash Privacy).
  • Google's Gemini AI (Data Controller): If you use AI features in the App, your input and the generated content are shared with Google's Gemini AI, where Google acts as a separate data controller. Please refer to Google's privacy policy for more information on how they handle your data (Google Gemini Privacy).
  • Legal Compliance: We may share your information if required by law or to protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users or the public.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. Our service providers also implement appropriate security measures, such as encryption at rest and in transit. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. User Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, such as the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of your data. To exercise these rights, please contact us at info@spacedcards.app.

For California residents, you have additional rights under the CCPA, including the right to know what personal information we collect, the right to request deletion of your personal information, and the right to opt-out of the sale of your personal information (though we do not sell personal information). Please see our CCPA section below for more details.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide the App's services or as required by law. When you delete your account, we will delete your personal information from our systems, except where we are required to retain it for legal or regulatory purposes.

7. Cookies and Tracking

Our App may use tracking technologies, such as mobile advertising identifiers or other unique identifiers, to collect information about your use of the App. This information is used to understand how you interact with the App and to improve its features and user experience. You can manage your tracking preferences through your device settings.

8. Third-Party Links

The App may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties and recommend that you review their privacy policies.

9. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it.

10. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the updated policy on our website or through the App. Your continued use of the App after such changes will constitute your acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at info@spacedcards.app.

CCPA Compliance for California Residents

If you are a California resident, you have certain rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). These rights include:

  • The right to know what personal information we collect, use, share, or sell about you.
  • The right to request that we delete any of your personal information that we have collected from you.
  • The right to opt-out of the sale of your personal information (though we do not sell personal information).

To exercise these rights, please contact us at info@spacedcards.app or by mail at [address].

We do not sell your personal information to third parties for monetary consideration.

We may share your personal information with our service providers who act as data processors on our behalf, as described in Section 3 above.

Categories of Personal Information Collected

  • Identifiers (name, email address)
  • Customer records (usage information)
  • Device information
  • Internet or other similar network activity information
  • Geolocation data (from IP address)
  • Audio, electronic, visual, or similar information (if applicable)
  • Professional or employment-related information (if applicable)
  • Education information (if applicable)
  • Inferences drawn from any of the above to create a profile or for any other purpose

We collect this information for the purposes described in Section 2 above.

Exercising Your CCPA Rights

To exercise your rights under the CCPA, please submit a request to info@spacedcards.app with the subject line "CCPA Request." In your request, please specify which right you are exercising and provide sufficient information to identify yourself and the personal information you are requesting.

We will respond to your request within the timeframes specified by the CCPA, typically within 45 days of receipt of the request.

If you have any questions or need assistance with your request, please contact us at info@spacedcards.app.

No Discriminatory Practices

We do not discriminate against you for exercising any of your rights under the CCPA. This means we will not:

  • Deny you goods or services.
  • Charge you different prices or rates for goods or services, including through the use of discounts or other benefits or imposing penalties.
  • Provide you a different level or quality of goods or services.
  • Suggest that you may receive a different price or rate for goods or services or a different level or quality of goods or services.

Opt-Out of Sale

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. Therefore, you do not have the right to opt-out of the sale of your personal information under the CCPA.

Important Note on AI Features

When using AI features in the App, please be aware that your input and the generated content are processed by Google's Gemini AI. Google acts as a separate data controller for this processing. We recommend that you do not input any sensitive or confidential information into the AI features, as this information may be used by Google to improve their AI models.

UpStash for Rate Limiting

We use UpStash to manage rate limiting, which helps prevent abuse of our services. For this purpose, UpStash may collect your IP address and other technical information to track and limit the number of requests from your device. Please refer to UpStash's privacy policy for more information on their data practices (UpStash Privacy).

Do Not Track

Our App does not currently recognize or respond to "Do Not Track" signals from mobile devices. However, we respect your privacy preferences and provide options to manage tracking through your device settings.
